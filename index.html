FAQ

Common questions.

Simple answers for students and schools.

What can flight school loans cover?

They may be used for qualifying flight training programs and related training costs, subject to review and approval.

Do I need a cosigner?

Some applicants may benefit from a cosigner depending on credit profile, program cost, and underwriting criteria.

Do you work with schools nationwide?

Yes. FlightSchoolLoan.com is being built to support student pilots and partner schools across the United States.

How fast is the initial review?

The goal is to provide a clean first step and a quick review timeline for qualified applicants.

Is the calculator an approval?

No. The calculator is for informational purposes only and does not represent final underwriting or a loan offer.

Can I start with no FAA medical yet?

Potentially, yes. That depends on program requirements, lender criteria, and where you are in the training process.
